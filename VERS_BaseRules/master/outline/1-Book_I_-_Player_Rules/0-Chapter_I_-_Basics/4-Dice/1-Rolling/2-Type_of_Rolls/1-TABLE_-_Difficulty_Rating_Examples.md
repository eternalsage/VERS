**Difficulty Rating Examples**

| DR	| Meaning			| Example				|
| -------- | -------------------------------| ----------------------------------------- |
| 0	| Extremely Easy		| Walking on a paved, flat road		|
| 1-2	| Easy			| Running across a rocky field		|
