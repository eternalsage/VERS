title:          Long Spear
ID:             997
type:           md
compile:        2



Designed for facing down charging cavalry, these spears are far too long for use in typical combat otherwise, often being 3 meters long or more. No horse would charge into a line of these set against them, and they were often deployed as a surprise tactic, being raised just before impact. (+4 Damage: 12; Set: 2; +2 Reach: 4; +1 Size: -1; Required Hands: -2) 15 CP.
